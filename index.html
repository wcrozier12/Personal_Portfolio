About Me

Graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Chemistry and Biology from Northern Arizona University. My work in the lab, at CVS, and with Maricopa county exposed me to the radical impact that software and websites have on all fields. I began coding on my own and found a passion in web development. I want to develop software to help people and improve people's lives.

I am currently enrolled in the University of Arizona Full Stack Software Development program. I continue to improve my skills and mastery daily in various languages including HTML, CSS, and Javascript. My plans are to become fluent in these and more languages to lead me into a career in Web Development.

When I'm not coding I enjoy traveling, backpacking, taking photos, and spending time with my dog and girlfriend. I also really have a passion for music and I love discovering new artists.
